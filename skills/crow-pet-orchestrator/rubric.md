# Evaluation Rubric

Pass at 8.0 / 10.

| Dimension | Weight | What It Measures |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Task clarity | 2 | Goal and success criteria are explicit. |
| Policy control | 2 | Risk and approvals are handled correctly. |
| Adapter selection | 1.5 | Runtime choice stays outside skills. |
| Delegation quality | 1.5 | Subagent brief is bounded and verifiable. |
| Handoff quality | 1.5 | Result includes evidence and artifacts. |
| Memory hygiene | 1.5 | Durable learnings are recorded without drift. |
