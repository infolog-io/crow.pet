# Evaluation Rubric

Pass at 8.0 / 10.

| Dimension | Weight | What It Measures |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Audience fit | 2 | Message matches the buyer and channel. |
| Brand fit | 2 | Voice and design references are followed. |
| Clarity | 2 | Offer and next step are clear. |
| Evidence | 2 | Claims are supportable. |
| Reuse value | 2 | Output can seed future examples. |
