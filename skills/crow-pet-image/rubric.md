# Evaluation Rubric

Pass at 8.0 / 10.

| Dimension | Weight | What It Measures |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Brief fit | 2 | Output matches content and format. |
| Brand fit | 2 | Brand rules are followed when present. |
| Technical quality | 2 | Resolution, crop, and artifacts are acceptable. |
| Safety | 2 | Claims and usage are safe. |
| Traceability | 2 | Prompt and output paths are recorded. |
