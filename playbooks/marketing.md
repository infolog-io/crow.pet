# Marketing Playbook

Reusable marketing lessons for public-safe crow.pet work.

## STRATEGIES & INSIGHTS

[specificity-0001] helpful=1 harmful=0 :: Describe concrete product behavior before using category labels like "agent OS" or "context engineering system."

[audience-first-0001] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Name who the artifact is for before choosing tone, density, or visual style.

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

[hype-0001] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Avoid broad claims such as "revolutionary" unless supported by a concrete behavior or artifact.
