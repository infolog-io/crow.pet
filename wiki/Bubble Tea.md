# Bubble Tea

A TUI framework for Go based on The Elm Architecture. With 41.2k stars and 1.2k forks, it is [[Charmbracelet]]'s most popular project.

## Ecosystem

- [[Bubbles]] — Pre-built TUI components designed for Bubble Tea.
- [[Lip Gloss]] — Styling library used to define layouts and colors.
- [[Wish]] — Extends Bubble Tea apps over SSH.
